El Pollo Loco Guide

Story

Help Pepe on his adventure through the Mexican desert. Face dangerous chickens and collect valuable items on your journey to defeat the mighty boss chicken.

Objective

Your goal is to navigate through the desert landscape, collect coins and bottles, defeat enemies along the way, and ultimately face the boss chicken in an epic final battle.

Gameplay Tips

Collect bottles to use as weapons against enemies. Jump on smaller enemies to defeat them safely. Watch your health bar and collect items to stay strong. The boss chicken becomes more aggressive when damaged.

How to Play

Movement

  • LEFT/RIGHT Arrow or A/D - Move character
  • UP Arrow or W or SPACE - Jump

Actions

  • D - Throw bottles at enemies
  • H - Toggle collision boxes (debug)

Please rotate your device

This game can only be played in landscape mode.
