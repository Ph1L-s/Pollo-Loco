⚙️ Game Options

El Pollo Loco Guide

Objective

Help Pepe defeat the boss chicken and collect items

Movement Controls

Arrow Keys LEFT/RIGHT - Move character

Arrow Key UP or SPACE - Jump

WASD Keys - Alternative movement (W/Space jump, A/D move)

Action Controls

D Key - Throw bottles at enemies

H Key - Toggle collision boxes (debug mode)

Gameplay

Collect bottles to use as weapons

Jump on enemies to defeat them

Watch your health and item counters

Defeat the boss to win

🐔 How to Play El Pollo Loco

🎮 Controls

  • ←→Move left/right
  • SPACEJump on enemies or over obstacles
  • FThrow bottles at enemies

🎯 Objectives

  • 🪙 Collect coins scattered throughout the world
  • 🍾 Gather bottles to use as projectiles
  • 🐓 Defeat normal chickens by jumping on them or throwing bottles
  • 🐔 Face the boss chicken with special attack patterns
  • 🏆 Win by defeating the boss chicken!

💡 Tips

  • 🎯 Jump on enemies from above for safe kills
  • ⚡ Boss gets more aggressive when damaged
  • 💎 Enemies drop bottles when defeated (20% chance)
  • 🔔 Watch for boss alert animation before charge attacks
  • ❤️ Avoid side collisions with enemies to prevent damage
